Korean Beef Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • 1 head lettuce with large leaves

  • 1 lb lean ground beef

  • ½ cup brown sugar

  • ¼ cup soy sauce/tamari

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil; divided in half

  • 3 cloves garlic; minced/grated

  • ¼ teaspoon. ground ginger

  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional and to taste)

  • 1 bunch green onions; divided between white and green parts

  • 3 tablespoon rice wine vinegar

  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Recipe

  1. Combine brown sugar, soy sauce/tamari, ½ tablespoon sesame oil, garlic, ginger, rice wine vinegar, and red pepper flakes; set aside.

  2. Brown the ground beef in ½ tablespoon sesame oil and white part of green onions.

  3. Drain any rendered fat.

  4. Pour the sauce over the meat and allow it to reduce down; about 3-5 minutes.

  5. Serve in lettuce leaves or over rice. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
