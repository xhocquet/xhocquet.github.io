My Thoughts

Good Mods


Yet (not) Another Maps Pack

Known as YnAMP. Detailed new map additions.

Terra Mirabilis

Large rework of natural wonders.

Detailed Worlds

Adds a bit more flavor to continents instead of blobs.

Production Queue

Really solid quality-of-life enhancement. If you have a set production order for new cities, this saves you some time.

Sukritact's Great Blue Hole

Shout-out since I've been here in real life.
